Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour & Family

Situated on the main Shahkot Road, Nankana Family Restaurant & Bar B Q has been the heart of family dining in Nankana Sahib. We bring you the finest traditional Pakistani cuisine — from smoky, tender Bar B Q to rich, aromatic karahis — all served with warmth and love.

With an open-air environment, a dedicated family hall, and ample parking, we are the ideal destination for gatherings, celebrations, and everyday meals alike.
